Pastelitos Recipe

A Dominican pastry, often served as an appetizer at home get togethers.

Pastelitos are a very common starter made to treat guests before a dinner, especially for the holidays. The usual filling they come with is ground beef, usually seasoned and prepared with typical Dominican seasonings. Nevertheless, you can add as a filling anything you prefer.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg (medium), eggs and whites separated
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1Â½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus extra for working the dough
  • meat of any kind (usually beef or chicken) for filling

Steps

  1. Combine dough ingredients
  2. Form the dough
  3. Make disks
  4. Assemble 1 disk over another, with filling inbetween
  5. Deep fry pastelitos
